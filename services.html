Services

HASSLE FREE HOLIDAY

Dubai is a self-sufficient holiday destination with all the services and amenities needed for comfortable living and hassle-free holidays.

The town offers a full range of facilities and services related to transportation, education, accommodation, communication, health, security, home upkeep, and more. For tickets, bookings, and scheduling information, visitors may refer to the Info Center in Downtown or inquire at the hotel Guest Relations desk

Family Services

The wide range of activities and services available for children is evidence of the community’s
commitment to the happiness of its youngest residents and visitors.

Some activities are designed for the whole family’s participation, while other services provide a safe
environment where kids can have fun under professional supervision.
